About me

Wish Ansar was another fictional character from this book, but he's not. Hence forcing me to abide by the rules and stick to reality and add no fiction but portray the history of the person behind this damn pen.
The first thing that will come into anybody's mind after picking up romantic fiction. Does he have a girlfriend?
Ah, no-no, of course, no.
Well, Ansar is Twenty-four, from down south, lands of the temple, festival and the synonym for the word beauty - 'Tamil Nadu'. Works at Cognizant as a Web Developer.
He has written over 100+ articles for various news companies and websites. Has also conducted various international essay and quote writing competitions, Coauthored over 40+ anthologies and has published over 200+ poetries and 250+ quotes in various anthologies, magazines and social media platforms, also His poetries have got featured on Radio.
He is the co-founder of Escritor Explorers, which set the platform for young aspirants in various fields. He has written and voiced scripts for advertisements for a few companies (Zenith Defence Solution, Brain ' O' Brain and Sports clubs).
He has won Spectrum's budding writer award in the year 2021 and the Rising writer Award in the same year. 'You & The Rest of The World' is his debut novel.
