About me

Ansar Hussain is a highly accomplished web developer, currently working at Cognizant
Ansar has displayed his passion for writing through the creation of over 100+ articles, covering both technical and non-technical topics, for reputable news companies. Furthermore, he has organized and executed several international essay and quotes writing competitions, fostering creative expression among aspiring writers. Additionally, Ansar has co-authored over 40+ anthologies and published more than 200+ poetries and 250+ quotes across a range of anthologies, magazines, websites and social networks.
Recognizing the importance of nurturing young talent,He has established the platform called Escritor Explorers. This initiative aims to inspire and support young aspirants in various fields, helping them realize their potential and achieve their goals.
His outstanding contributions have been acknowledged and honored, as Ansar received the prestigious Spectrum's budding writer award in 2021, and was further recognized with the Rising Award in the same year. These accolades stand as a testament to his exceptional skills and dedication to his craft.
Notable among his literary endeavors is the compilation of the anthology titled "Pursuit of Love," a collection that showcases his ability to weave compelling narratives and evoke strong emotions.
Furthermore, Ansar is the esteemed author of the romantic fiction novel "You and the Rest of the World," which captivates readers with its poignant storytelling and compelling characters.
